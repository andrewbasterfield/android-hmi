# Quickstart: Widget Duplication

## Overview
Duplicate existing widgets to quickly build complex dashboards with consistent styling and configuration.

## How to Duplicate a Widget

1.  **Enter Edit Mode**: Toggle the "Edit Mode" button in the dashboard top bar.
2.  **Open Widget Settings**: Click on any widget to open its configuration dialog.
3.  **Duplicate**: Click the "Duplicate" button at the bottom of the dialog (next to the "Delete" button).
4.  **Confirm**: The dialog will close, and a new widget with identical settings will appear.
    - The duplicate will be positioned with a **(+1, +1)** grid offset from the original.
    - The new widget will automatically be selected, allowing you to move it immediately.
5.  **Reconfigure (Optional)**: If you need to change the PLC tag or label for the duplicate, click on it again to open its settings.

## Key Features
- **Inherited Configuration**: Labels, colors, and scaling are all preserved in the duplicate.
- **Auto Z-Order**: The new widget is always placed on top of existing components.
- **Immediate Persistence**: The dashboard is saved automatically after duplication.
